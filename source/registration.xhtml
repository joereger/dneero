Get started as a Blogger or as a Researcher
Sign Up is free. On this page we collect some basics and then send you an email message with a link in it to activate your account. Once activated, you'll log in and start working as a Blogger or as a Researcher (all accounts can do both functions).

Beta Mode
While we're in beta mode no real money will change hands. Researchers will be given a balance of $100,000 to use to create surveys and pay bloggers. Bloggers will accrue a balance but it will not be paid. At the end of the beta period all balances will be set to $0.
